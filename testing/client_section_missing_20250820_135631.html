Summary

Who we are

DexCare modernizes digital access and care orchestration so nobody waits for care. We serve leading health systems and their patients at scale, bringing operational clarity to complex environments.

Why this role exists

DexCare.ai is evolving into an intelligence layer that activates each customer’s own knowledge—protocols, decision trees, and frontline expertise—to guide staff and patients in real time. Our agents engage via voice, chat, and search, learn from every interaction, and are powered by each customer’s rules, preferences, and processes—not a generic model.

We’re hiring an engineer to lead the agentic stack that (1) interviews staff and SMEs to capture institutional knowledge, (2) turns that knowledge into grounded, auditable guidance, and (3) runs within or writes to the customer’s tenant with strict data isolation. You’ll build across Azure, Azure OpenAI (GPT‑4o) with speech‑to‑speech, and multi‑modal channels for web, phone, and SMS.

What you’ll do (outcomes over activities)

· Design & build an agentic AI service that interviews SMEs (async and live), generates clarifying questions when gaps are detected, and transforms validated answers into durable knowledge objects.

· Implement speech‑to‑speech and full‑duplex conversational flows (barge‑in, latency budgets, diarization) using Azure Speech/Realtime capabilities with GPT‑4o.

· Stand up multi‑modal channels: web chat, voice via calling, and SMS, integrating with Azure Communication Services/Bot Framework.

· Build a grounded reasoning pipeline—RAG with Azure AI Search and policy‑aware execution.

· Engineer tenant‑isolated data flows: per‑tenant storage, encryption, key management, RBAC, audit trails, and BYO subscription patterns.

· Ship evaluation & safety harnesses and instrument outcome metrics.

· Partner with Product/Design to deliver assistants for staff and care agents for consumers.

· Integrate with Microsoft 365 surfaces (Teams/Outlook) to capture SME knowledge in‑flow.

· Contribute to SRE‑level operability: tracing, rate‑limit strategies, cost controls, rollouts, and robust on‑call.

What you’ll bring (minimum qualifications)

· 4+ years building distributed systems or conversational AI at scale; 2+ years focused on LLM/agentic applications or speech systems.

· Deep experience with Azure (Functions or AKS), Azure OpenAI (GPT‑4o), Azure AI Speech, Azure AI Search, Key Vault, and modern eventing/queues.

· Real‑time, streaming conversational experience.

· Proficiency in TypeScript/Node.js or Python; strong API design.

· Hands‑on with RAG architectures and policy‑aware orchestration.

· Security and compliance mindset; comfort building in regulated environments.

Nice to have

· Telephony/IVR and Azure Communication Services (PSTN/SMS), or Bot Framework experience.

· Knowledge graph or workflow engines; evaluation tooling; streaming UX on the web.

· Prior healthcare experience (HIPAA), or complex multi‑tenant SaaS with customer‑tenant isolation.

· M365 integration experience.

How we’ll measure success (first 6–12 months)

· Time‑to‑knowledge: SME interview to validated knowledge object within defined SLA.

· Grounding accuracy and policy adherence in agent responses.

· First‑contact resolution and deflection rate for voice/chat flows.

· Tenant isolation verified through internal tests and customer security reviews.
